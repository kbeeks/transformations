An Ode to
British Panel Shows

This website is a tribute to the brilliance of the British Panel Show.
Highly competitive, but with no real prize or benefit to the winner...
they showcase the quirky, peculiar, and dry British wit at its best.

Featuring top comedians, they are the perfect viewing pleasure anytime (but even more so now) and every now and then you witness the collision of comedic brilliance/absolute insanity that results in moments of breath-stealing non-stop laughter (any time Bob Mortimer is on Would I Lie to You).

To dive deeper, give James Greeby's GQ article a read, The Chaotic Serenity of the UK Panel Show and visit the GALLERY (link at top of page) for my favourites!
